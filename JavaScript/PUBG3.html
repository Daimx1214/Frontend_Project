Update 40.2 — March 2026

PUBG 9th Anniversary

Nine years of battlegrounds. Drop in and celebrate with drone shows, Chicken Man hunts, and anniversary surprises scattered across every map.

STILL HERE, ALLDAY

Nine years of drops, victories, and legendary moments. The battlegrounds transform into a celebration — join the party before the final circle closes.

🎆
All Maps Normal & Ranked

Drone Fireworks Spectacular

Look to the skies! A spectacular drone show lights up the battlefield in celebration of PUBG's 9th Anniversary. Drone formations spell "PUB9" above Georgopol, adding brilliance to every match start.

Applies to: Normal Match, Ranked, and Custom Match — Normal Mode.

🗿
Starting Island All Maps

Party on the Battlegrounds

A special 9th Anniversary statue awaits on the starting island. Throw a Holographic Stun Grenade at the statue — after enough hits, it transforms into your character's appearance!

The statue and Holographic Stun Grenade can be found on the starting island of all maps. Applies to Normal Match and Ranked.

🐔
Rondo Only Lucky Eggs

Chicken Man Lucky Egg Hunt

A mysterious Chicken Man has been spotted throughout Rondo. Hunt down the runaway Chicken Man and secure a Lucky Egg as your reward!

Lucky Eggs unlock 1 of 13 random items: weapons, Level 3 gear, and the exclusive 9th Anniversary Ghillie Suit (Party).

💡 Lucky Eggs can also be obtained through the 9th Anniversary Draw at all three Rondo Pop-Up Store locations. Spin for your chance at exclusive loot!
🏪
Rondo 3 Locations

9th Anniversary Pop-Up Stores

Three 9th Anniversary Pop-Up Store locations have been placed across Rondo. Each features the Anniversary Draw — spin for Lucky Eggs and exclusive celebration rewards.

Applies to Normal Match, Ranked, and Custom Match — Normal Mode.

📦
All Maps

Decorated Supply Crates & Billboards

Containers and Care Packages across Rondo have been specially decorated for the anniversary. Commemorative billboards in collaboration with ALLDAY PROJECT have been added across the map.

Additionally, look above Rondo Stadium for a special gift from the Director. Anniversary celebration messages appear throughout all maps.

🎵
Collaboration K-Pop

ALLDAY PROJECT Collaboration

"STILL HERE, ALLDAY" — PUBG's 9th anniversary theme features a special K-pop collaboration with ALLDAY PROJECT, with themed billboards, branding, and celebration content woven throughout the battlegrounds.

Check the Rondo Stadium area for a special surprise prepared by the Director in celebration of this collaboration.

Item Spawn Rebalance

Vikendi — Area & Spawn Updates

Major changes to Vikendi's area design and item distribution in Update 40.2. Crowbar-locked doors have been replaced, secret rooms now offer expanded gear options, and cabinet item rates have been rebalanced across the entire map.

🚪

Crowbar Doors Removed

All locked doors originally designed to be opened with Crowbars have been replaced with standard doors. Crowbar storage and spawns have been removed.

🏠

Secret Room — New Item Spawns

9 new item types added to Secret Room spawn lists: Jammer Pack, Emergency Pickup, Folded Shield, Mountain Bike, Mortar, All-In-One Repair Kit, Stun Gun, Zipline Gun, Emergency Cover Flare.

🗄️

Cabinet Items Rebalanced

Overall item types and spawn rates from Cabinets across Vikendi have been adjusted. Applies to Normal Match, Ranked and Custom Match.

📦

General Item Spawn Rebalance

Item spawns have been rebalanced across the entire Vikendi map. Applies to both Normal Match and Custom Match — Normal Mode.

PC Only — Alpha Feature

UGC Alpha

Update 40.2 expands PUBG's User Generated Content Alpha with creator verification tools and new Subzero items for custom UGC modes.

Creator Information Feature Added

A new feature allows creators to designate and verify the original author of a mode file. The 'Edit Manager > Settings > Creator' tab has been added, providing full creator metadata control.

The previous 'Edit Manager > Load > CREATOR tab' has been removed and replaced with the new unified Creator Settings system.

Bug Fixes

Update 40.2 addresses a range of gameplay, world, UI, and cosmetic issues. Click any category to expand the full list of fixes.

🎮

Gameplay

7 Fixes ▲
✓Fixed Smoke Grenade's smoke color changing abruptly based on character position.
✓Fixed watching an AI group emote on the starting island not ending properly — emote BGM continued playing after AI stopped.
✓Fixed placing an Emergency Pickup in terrain gaps placing it below the surface and failing to call the plane.
✓Fixed playing your own emote while 'Join / Watch Group Emote' with another player's emote resulting in both emote BGMs playing simultaneously.
✓Fixed Folded Shield skin not applied when obtained via 'Load Gear Set' in Training mode.
✓Fixed "Eject" interaction not appearing if Recall flight path was in a non-playable area when returning via Blue Chip registration.
✓(PC) Fixed prone players being momentarily displayed as floating in midair.
🗺️

World

3 Fixes ▼
✓Fixed collision, texture, performance, and various general Vikendi issues.
✓Fixed the mini-map in the Boatyard area of Erangel in Team Deathmatch mode incorrectly displaying as the Erangel: Subzero mini-map.
🖥️

UX / UI

1 Fix ▼
✓(PC) Fixed changes made in 'Settings – Key Bindings – Vehicle' not being reflected in the Key Guide.
👗

Items & Skins

5 Fixes ▼
✓Fixed the back of character's head becoming transparent when wearing the Aston Martin Jacket with the Snowbound Mask.
✓Fixed male character's ankle becoming transparent when wearing XingHun's Tiger Starfire Cargo Pants with Snow Angel Boots.
✓Fixed character's thigh becoming partially transparent when wearing Ailee's Leggings with certain pants.
✓Fixed clipping issue on character's waist when wearing Toon Raider Denim Pants with certain tops.
